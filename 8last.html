Highest Mountains in the World

The tallest mountains in the world are found in Asia, primarily in the Himalayas and the Karakoram range. The highest peak is Mount Everest (8,848 m / 29,029 ft).

  • Mount Everest – 8,848 m
  • K2 – 8,611 m
  • Kangchenjunga – 8,586 m
  • Lhotse – 8,516 m
  • Makalu – 8,485 m
  • Cho Oyu – 8,188 m
  • Dhaulagiri – 8,167 m
  • Manaslu – 8,163 m
